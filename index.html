Research
Bachelor's Dissertation · 04/2026

The Impact of News Sentiment on Daily Trading Volumes and Stock Prices: The Case of Apple Inc.

Examining how daily news sentiment drive market reaction, relfected in the variations in daily stock exchange of Apple Inc.. Drawing on 10 months of historical trading data, I found that news has adverse effects on daily trading volume and prices, and proposed that sentiment should be considered in algorithmic trading. In addition, a new hybrid sentiment index was proposed as a solid measure for news sentiment for frequent trading strategies.
